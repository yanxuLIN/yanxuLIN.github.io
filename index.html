About practice

We are here to add what we can to life, not to get what we can from it. 

2014--2016

When I first entered the university, I entered the college journalist group. In this warm society, I am responsible for interviews, manuscript writing, and newspaper publishing. Because of my personal hobbies, I joined the school radio station. After the selection, I became a school radio announcer.

2016--2018

During these two years, I was responsible for the operation of the school's official WeChat public account. The content of my work mainly includes planning topics, writing articles and photography. That was my first exposure to new media, but for me it just changed the way an article was written and published.

2018--2019

The writing experience I accumulated in the school began to enter social consumption. I started to manage new media accounts for some companies and government departments. I interviewed the industrious and dedicated middle school principals and also met with the calligraphy training teachers who gradually became commercialized. These experiences have made me think that the world is diverse.
